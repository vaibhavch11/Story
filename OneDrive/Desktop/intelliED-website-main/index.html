What is IntelliED?




IntelliED is a light-weight digital platform that provides Easy Data Management Analytics for schools, allowing them to improve the efficiency of grading and reporting.

Why Choose IntelliED?

Automated Reports

All you have to do is enter the data points and then our AI-ML models & Data Science frameworks will generate insights & reports automatically, saving time by eliminating the need to develop reports manually. With just a few clicks, you can share these reports with students, counsellors, parents & school admins.

Data Driven Decision Making

Our Machine Learning & Analytics-based approach brings the most meaningful insights to the table so that you can provide precise feedback & can make effective & timely interventions to support students struggling in the classroom, thereby promoting a data-driven decision-making environment.

Social Emotional Learning

Schools can offer our built-in Social-Emotional-Learning surveys & Psychometric tests to their students to ensure that their environments are safe, supportive, and equitable & can profile students based on their learning styles and personality types.

Better Classroom Management

Efficiently track academic progress, record daily attendance, provide important announcements, and share learning resources with anyone instantly. IntelliED eliminates the use of paper, reduces human reporting errors, and streamlines your day-to-day administrative tasks.
